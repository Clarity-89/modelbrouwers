{% trans "Rules & guidelines" %}

{% blocktrans %}

You can submit your group build ideas here, find interested people, and tweak the subject/data as much as you want.

When you're happy with the result, check the calendar and make sure your start-date/duration are reasonable. Then you can submit the group build to the board moderators.

The moderators will then review the group build and assign a start date and duration. If needed, they will communicate to you about potential changes.

Some general rules apply. The staff can always decide to divert from them if there's a reason for diversion.

  • No more than one group build per discipline. A discipline is roughly equal with a forum (like cars, military aviation, …)
  • A 'minimum' of 10 participants. Make sure your potential participants have subscribed to the group build, this will be used to check if the quota are met. 'Minimum' is quoted, because 9 participants is usually not a big issue.
  • Think about the duration and make a reasonable estimate. The default duration is three months, this is for a reason. Extensions are possible, but you need a strong case to get it - especially if different group builds in the same forum section are planned!
  • Group builds can be closed at any time if the activity died out. It's your responsibility as admin/gb moderator to keep it active and interesting.
  • Any other rules (like starting with an unoped kit, amount of kits you can participante with, …) are up to the organisers. Please take care to mention these rules the rules field or in your rules topic.

And most of all, have fun! Group builds are a valuable and popular aspect of scale modelling communities!
